Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a parking space?

To book a parking space, search for available spaces, select one, and follow the booking process.

How do I list my parking space?

Go to the "List Parking" section in your dashboard and provide the required details.

What are the payment methods available?

We accept credit/debit cards, net banking, and UPI payments for bookings.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes, you can cancel a booking via the "My Bookings" section. Cancellation fees may apply.
